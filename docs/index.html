From Simulated Brains to Walking Bodies​

Welcome to the companion website for Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics, a comprehensive practitioner textbook covering the modern 2025-2027 embodied AI stack.

What You'll Learn​

  • ROS 2 Fundamentals: Master the Robot Operating System for humanoid control
  • Simulation Mastery: Create realistic digital twins with NVIDIA Isaac Sim
  • Edge Deployment: Deploy AI models on NVIDIA Jetson Orin edge hardware
  • Vision-Language-Action Models: Integrate cutting-edge VLA models for manipulation
  • Bipedal Locomotion: Implement walking controllers and whole-body control
  • End-to-End Integration: Build a voice-controlled autonomous humanoid system

Target Audience​

  • Advanced undergraduate and Master's students in robotics and AI
  • Industry engineers transitioning to humanoid robotics roles
  • University instructors teaching robotics courses
  • Researchers seeking reproducible baselines

Book Structure​

The book is organized into 6 parts covering 21 chapters:

  1. Foundations & ROS 2 (Chapters 1-4)
  2. Digital Twins & Simulation (Chapters 5-8)
  3. Perception & Edge Brain (Chapters 9-12)
  4. Embodied Cognition & VLA Models (Chapters 13-16)
  5. Bipedal Locomotion & Control (Chapters 17-19)
  6. Capstone Integration (Chapters 20-21)
